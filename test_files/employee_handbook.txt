EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK — NEXUS TECHNOLOGIES

WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9am to 6pm Monday through Friday. Employees may work flexible hours with manager approval. Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week. All employees are expected to be reachable during core hours of 10am to 4pm regardless of location.

LEAVE POLICY
Employees receive 20 days of paid annual leave per year, accruing at 1.67 days per month. Unused leave up to 10 days may be carried over to the next calendar year. Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days per year. Parental leave is 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers and 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted twice yearly — in June and December. Employees are rated on a 5-point scale across four dimensions: delivery, collaboration, growth, and leadership. A rating of 3 or above is considered meeting expectations. Ratings of 1 or 2 trigger a 90-day performance improvement plan (PIP).

COMPENSATION
Salaries are reviewed annually in January. Merit increases are based on performance ratings and market benchmarking. Employees rated 5 receive a merit increase of 10-15%. Employees rated 4 receive 5-8%. Employees rated 3 receive 2-4%. Ratings below 3 receive no increase.

CODE OF CONDUCT
Employees must treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind is grounds for immediate termination. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR in writing. Outside employment must be approved by the employee's direct manager.

DATA AND SECURITY
Employees must not share company data with unauthorized parties. All devices used for work must have full-disk encryption enabled. Passwords must be at least 16 characters and stored in the company-approved password manager. Employees who lose a work device must report it to IT within 2 hours.

TERMINATION
Employees may resign with 2 weeks written notice. The company may terminate employment immediately for cause, including gross misconduct, theft, or repeated policy violations. Employees terminated without cause receive severance equal to 2 weeks of base salary per year of service, up to a maximum of 26 weeks.
